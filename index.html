About: Mapping the young adults of New Zealand

This interactive map depicts the population change of New Zealand's young adults between 2001 and 2013. The original density dot map was created by Aaron Schiff using census data:

"[…] there is one blue dot for each new person in census area units that experienced population growth over this time, and one red dot for each person lost in areas where the population shrank."

In addition to population changes of young adults "Aged 20 - 34", you can select "All ages" for comparison.
